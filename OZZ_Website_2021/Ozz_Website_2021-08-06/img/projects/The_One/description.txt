Steps away from the most popular restaurants in Toronto, endless transit options and everything else you love about Toronto sits The One Condos at 1 Bloor West.  
Boasting 416 suites. The One Condos will become the heart and soul of the city. 
Sitting at a stunning 94 storeys tall, this building will become the second tallest in Canada next to the CN tower.

Ozz partnered with Mizrahi Developments and is proud to be a part in the creation of such a monumental structure.
